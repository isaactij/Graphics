This project is a game in which you dodge some enemies and shoot others in order to reach a final destination, which is the victory condition. The project uses a mario theme. In order to move, use the arrow keys, and press up to jump. In order to shoot fireballs, press 'f'. In order to win the game, make it to the flag in the end. Jump to hit the bottom of platforms to knock coins out and raise your score. Killing enemies also raises your score.

Press 'p' to pause
press 'v' to toggle background music
